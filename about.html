Who I Am

Hi, I'm Subash

I'm a student currently working my way through full stack web development. I got into it out of curiosity about how websites actually work, and it's turned into something I genuinely spend a lot of time on.

I'm still learning, but I've come a long way from the basics. I can now build complete pages with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, and I'm starting to explore the back-end with PHP.

This portfolio is basically my learning journal. Every topic has a live demo so you can actually play with the concepts which is way more fun than just reading about them.

Learning Journey

My Timeline

Early 2025
First HTML Page
Wrote my very first HTML page which was a simple "Hello World" that barely worked. Realised the web was more interesting than I thought.
Mid 2025
CSS & Styling
Discovered Flexbox and spent way too long centering a div. Started understanding the box model and why spacing matters.
Late 2025
Bootstrap & Responsive Grids
Bootstrap made responsive layouts click for me. Built my first multi-column page that actually looked good on mobile.
Early 2026
JavaScript & DOM
JavaScript was a jump. Went from styling to actual logic — event listeners, DOM manipulation, and making pages interactive.
2025 – Present
Full Stack & PHP
Starting to connect the front end to a server. Learning PHP, forms that actually send data, and how databases fit in. This portfolio is part of that journey.
